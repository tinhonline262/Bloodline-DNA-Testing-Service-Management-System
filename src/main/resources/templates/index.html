Frequently asked questions ?

We have shared some of the most frequently asked questions about our DNA testing services to help you out.

Our DNA paternity tests are 99.9% accurate when determining paternity and 100% accurate when excluding paternity. We use state-of-the-art laboratory equipment and analyze up to 21 genetic markers to ensure the highest level of accuracy. All tests are performed in AABB-accredited laboratories following strict quality control procedures.

Standard processing time is 3-5 business days from when we receive your samples at our laboratory. We also offer expedited processing for urgent cases, which can deliver results within 1-2 business days for an additional fee. You'll receive an email notification as soon as your results are ready, and you can access them securely through your online account.

We offer a comprehensive range of DNA testing services including Paternity Testing, Maternity Testing, Sibling Testing, Grandparent Testing, Aunt/Uncle Testing, and Immigration DNA Testing. We also provide specialized tests for twin zygosity, Y-chromosome lineage testing, and mitochondrial DNA analysis. All tests are available for both legal and personal knowledge purposes.

Not at all! Our sample collection process is completely painless and non-invasive. We use buccal (cheek) swabs that simply require gently rubbing the inside of your cheek for about 30 seconds. The process is safe for people of all ages, including newborns. The collection kit comes with detailed instructions, and our customer service team is available to guide you through the process if needed.

Absolutely! We take your privacy very seriously. All test results are strictly confidential and are only released to authorized individuals who ordered the test. We use advanced encryption technology to protect your data, and our laboratories follow HIPAA compliance guidelines. Your DNA samples are destroyed after testing unless you specifically request storage. We will never share your information with third parties without your explicit consent.

Yes, we offer legal DNA testing that is admissible in court proceedings. Legal tests require a strict chain of custody process, photo identification, and witness verification during sample collection. These tests cost slightly more than personal knowledge tests but provide legally defensible results for paternity disputes, child support cases, custody battles, and immigration applications. Our legal reports are accepted by courts, government agencies, and legal professionals nationwide.

We can perform posthumous DNA testing using various types of samples from the deceased person, including hair with roots, fingernail clippings, razor blades, toothbrushes, or tissue samples. We also offer discrete DNA testing using personal items. Additionally, we can test close relatives (parents, siblings, children) to establish family relationships when direct testing isn't possible. Please contact our specialists to discuss the best approach for your specific situation.

Our personal knowledge paternity tests start at $199, while legal paternity tests start at $299. Sibling and grandparent tests range from $349-$449. We offer payment plans and package deals for multiple participants. All tests include the collection kit, laboratory analysis, and comprehensive results report. We also provide free consultations to help you choose the right test for your needs and budget.

Legal requirements vary by jurisdiction. For personal knowledge testing, we generally require consent from the child's legal guardian. For legal testing, we may require consent from both parents or a court order. We recommend consulting with a family law attorney if you're facing custody or consent issues. Our customer service team can also help explain the requirements in your specific situation and provide guidance on obtaining proper authorization.

Inconclusive results are extremely rare (less than 1% of cases) and usually occur due to insufficient DNA in the sample or genetic mutations. If this happens, we will retest your samples at no additional charge. If the retest is still inconclusive, we may recommend additional genetic markers or different family members to achieve a conclusive result. We stand behind our testing and will work with you until we reach a definitive answer.

We specialize in immigration DNA testing for visa applications and family reunification cases. Our team can coordinate with embassies, consulates, and international legal representatives. Additional shipping and handling fees apply for international orders, and processing times may vary depending on customs and shipping methods.

HOW IT WORKS

Simple, Fast & Accurate DNA Testing Process

Our streamlined DNA testing process ensures maximum convenience and accuracy. Just follow these simple steps to get the answers you need.

1
Order the Kit

Choose the type of test you need and order online. We'll ship the sample collection kit to you within 24 hours, with free delivery.

2
Collect the Sample

Use the provided painless cheek swab. Follow the simple instructions to collect DNA samples from all participants.

3
Send Back the Sample

Send your sample back to our lab using the prepaid shipping label. You can track your package all the way to our accredited laboratory.

4
Receive Your Results

Get your secure results within 3–5 business days via encrypted email and through our online portal.
